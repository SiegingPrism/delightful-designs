# FlowSphere — Vision

## One-line pitch
**FlowSphere is a Life RPG for productivity** — a single dark, glassy command center where tasks, habits, focus, health, and AI coaching combine into one self-reinforcing growth loop.

## The problem
Modern productivity is fractured:
- **Notion** is a blank canvas — powerful but cold, requires you to build the system.
- **Todoist** does tasks well but ignores habits, energy, and recovery.
- **Habitica** gamifies habits but feels childish and isolated from real work.
- **Apple Health / Fitbit** track the body but don't talk to your work.

The result: people bounce between 4–6 apps, lose context, lose streaks, and lose motivation. None of them adapt to *you*.

## The solution
One adaptive system with three layers:

1. **Capture layer** — tasks, habits, focus sessions, health logs, mood.
2. **Game layer** — XP, levels, streaks, skill trees, unlockable themes & AI boosts.
3. **Intelligence layer** — an AI Coach (Lovable AI Gateway / Gemini) that reads your patterns and proposes the next right move: a task, a break, a lighter day.

Everything is one tap away. Everything earns XP. Everything feeds the coach.

## Why now
- LLMs are finally cheap and fast enough to run a personal coach.
- Burnout is a measurable cultural problem — productivity tools that ignore it are obsolete.
- Gen Z and millennials grew up on game loops; spreadsheet productivity feels alien to them.

## North-star metric
**Daily Active Streak Length** — the median number of consecutive days a user opens the app *and* completes ≥1 meaningful action (task, focus session, or habit check-in).

If users come back daily, the loop is working.

## Non-goals
- Team collaboration (v1 is single-player).
- Project management (no Gantt charts, no dependencies).
- Calendar replacement (we read calendars, we don't host them).

## Three-year vision
FlowSphere becomes the default "operating system for your day" for self-directed people — students, indie hackers, creators, knowledge workers — replacing the Notion + Todoist + Habitica + Apple Health stack with one adaptive surface.
